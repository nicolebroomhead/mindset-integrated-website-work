The Future of Legal Case Management

Current legal software services are designed to address two distinct needs: eDiscovery (electronic compilation and search of relevant records) and cloud-based case management (tracking casework, billing, and calendars). Neither of these have built-in systems for organizing and utilizing the vast amounts of stored records throughout the lifecycle of a case. 

MINDSET's CaseReview® is the first customizable and synergistic legal and medical file case management platform for groundbreaking understanding and collaboration.

Intuitive, low-cost organization of your files helps to uncover actionable the actionable information you need most. Our team of experienced professionals is available to perform complete, unbiased file review.
