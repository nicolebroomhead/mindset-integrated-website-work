The MINDSET Team

Meet our team of dedicated experts driving scientific breakthroughs to deliver greater impact with objective brain imaging data through real-world applications.

Chris N Airriess, Ph.D.

Chief Executive Officer

Dr. Airriess has a long history of connecting business and science in the development of MedTech solutions. In his previous role as CEO of Cortechs.ai, he drove strategic development of the company that pioneered the application of volumetric MRI within routine clinical practice. Prior to Cortechs.ai, as COO of California Stem Cell (acquired by Caladrius Biosciences), Dr. Airriess drove company development from start-up to the clinical development phase of several therapeutic candidates, including FDA approval to begin a Phase III clinical trial. 

Jason Kerkmans, J.D., CBIS

Founder & EVP | Strategic Development

Jason identified the scalable potential of MINDSET’s scientific analysis. Appointed to the New Mexico Brain Injury Advisory Council (BIAC) and the New Mexico Supreme Court’s Rules of Evidence Committee, he has worked on legal briefings in state, military, and federal courts including the US Supreme Court.      

Rebecca Shore, M.A., J.D.

Vice President of Operations

Rebecca brings years of experience and expertise to the legal teams and clients working with MINDSET. Her degrees in Science, Special Education, and Law, and institutional knowledge as an integral part of Mindset Consulting, the company’s prior iteration, provide Rebecca with a remarkable dexterity in managing MINDSET’s Case Consultation and CaseReview services. 

Jules Fisher

Chief Regulatory & Quality Officer

Previously involved in four successful start-up companies as well as high-growth life science companies, Jules is a  healthcare industry veteran with over 25 years experience working with industry majors such as Smith & Nephew, Aventis Quintiles, and alongside companies such as Novartis and Pfizer.

Nithya Ramakrishnan, M.S.

Director of Product Development

As Director of Product, Nithya brings a wealth of experience in prioritizing features and delivering tailored solutions to customer needs. With a strong background in biomedical engineering and neuroscience, she excels in leading cross-functional teams, emphasizing data-driven decision-making and strategic partnerships for driving innovation within the neurotech sector.

Vikram Rao, M.S.

Head of R&D | Senior Scientific Programmer

Olivia Advincula, M.S.
